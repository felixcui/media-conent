# Design System — Codex 画框架图 HyperFrames Video

## Mood
清爽、技术编辑感、像一张可以被慢慢展开的纸面架构草图。不是霓虹科技，也不是纯 PPT；重点是“结构被快速提炼出来”的过程感。

## Palette
- Background paper: `#F4F1E8`
- Foreground ink: `#121826`
- Muted ink: `#4B5565`
- Panel: `#FFFDF6`
- Hairline: `#C9BEA8`
- Accent orange: `#D45500`
- Accent blue: `#2F4A7F`
- Accent teal: `#0F766E`
- Accent yellow: `#E4B649`

## Typography
- Display: generic `serif`. Use 800-900 weight and tight tracking for headlines.
- Body: generic `sans-serif`. Use 360-520 weight.
- Technical labels: `JetBrains Mono`, fallback monospace. Use tabular numbers.

## Corners and depth
- Large panels: 34-44px radius.
- Small tags/cards: 18-28px radius.
- Shadows: warm paper shadows, never harsh black.

## Avoid
- No neon cyan/purple gradients.
- No default Inter/Roboto.
- No centered equal-weight slide layouts.
- No flat white backgrounds; keep paper tint and visible texture.
